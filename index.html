Can Dragons fly?

Why yes, in fact they can. Except they are not real.

Can Wizards fly?

Unlike dragons, wizards cannot fly. Instead they use their staves in a pogo-stick-like fashion to propel themselves great distances.

Can Dwarves fly?

Dwarves are known to hide their wings in their beards, only exposing them when they need to flee or if they must travel long distances. Because dwarves spend so much time underground, their wings are rarely seen.
